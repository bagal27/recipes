Ingredients:
 - 1 cup water
 - 3 cups flour
 - 2 T oil
 - 2 T sugar
 - 1 1/2 t salt
 - 2 1/4 t yeast

Directions:
  1. Take the pan out of the bread machine, make sure it has the paddle.
  2. Add all ingredients to the pan.
  3. Place pan in bread machine.
  4. Make sure the default selection is bread.
  5. Select crust as L (for light).
  6. Press start.
  7. Bread will be done in 3 hours.
